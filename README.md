# Andes Retail Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the commercial performance of **Andes Retail Group** during **2024–2025** using Power BI.  
The objective of the dashboard is to provide an executive view of the business and allow deeper exploration of sales performance across countries, product categories, customer segments, and seasonal trends.

## Business Questions

The dashboard was designed to answer the following questions:

- How has total revenue evolved between 2024 and 2025?
- Which customer segments generate the highest revenue and profitability?
- Which product categories have the greatest impact on the business?
- Are there relevant differences between countries?
- Are there seasonal patterns in sales performance?

## Dashboard Structure

### 1. Overview
Provides a quick executive summary of the business performance, including:

- Total revenue
- Profitability
- Sales volume
- Revenue trends over time
- Revenue comparison across countries and product categories

### 2. Detail View
Allows deeper analysis to identify patterns and opportunities:

- Revenue by customer segment
- Profitability by country
- Revenue by season
- Summary table with key commercial metrics

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- Data Visualization
